Mac and Cheese Wings

Description

Mac and Cheese Wings are a unique and indulgent twist on classic chicken wings. This recipe combines the creamy comfort of mac and cheese with the irresistible crispy texture of chicken wings, resulting in a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cup flour
  • ¼ cup cheddar powder
  • 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ¾ cup whole milk
  • 1 whole egg
  • ½ cup shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 10-15 party wings
  • 3 cups large macaroni noodles, precooked and drained
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 dash worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp cheddar powder
  • 3 green onions

Steps

  1. Whisk together all the dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk together all the wet ingredients. Mix the wet and dry mixtures.
  3. Toss the wings into the spice mixture.
  4. Dip into pancake batter.
  5. Quickly dip into a sheet pan full of macaroni noodles.
  6. Deep fry until golden brown.
  7. Finish in the oven until the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Combine the buffalo sauce, butter, and heavy cream in a small sauce pot.
  9. Toss in the cheddar cheese, parmesan, cheddar powder and stir.
  10. Squeeze onto wings. Top with sliced green onions.
  11. ENJOY!
